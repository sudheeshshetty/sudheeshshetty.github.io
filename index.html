About

Hi, my name is Sudheesh Shetty. I am from Mangalore. I finished my education there. I always loved technologies. But I was not a guy who explores. I was introduced to Free Software Movement Karnataka(FSMK) in 2012 which is when I was taught to explore things and learn on my own. I was introduced to open source technologies there. It was kind of turning point in my life

I finished my Engineering from Vivekananda College of Engineering and Technology, Puttur under Visveshwaraya Technology in the year 2104. I started working in Persistent Systems Ltd, Pune from Nov 2014. Started as a backend developer there and later was introduced to full stack development. I love working on my free time and to explore new things in the technologies I learnt. I built few projects during my part time which is always available on github. I also started writing about what I learnt. I have always been part of FSMK camps later mentoring new students and helping them to find their interest in technology.

I am that guy who also likes to keep myself fit. I do gym and exercises to keep myself fit. Tripping and trecking are the things that no one would hate and neither do I. I have been to many places with my friends. If some one is speaking of technology, Open Source that would definetly take my attention.
